Program Sections
Hockey clubs provide their services and activities for different ages and groups.

Junior Program

The program is available to high school students and graduates who seek a greater or different challenge than that which might be available through their prep school team, high school varsity or club team, or area 18 & under teams. It for players who are aspire to play professional level.

Teenager Program

Teen hockey programs are designed to provide young players with the opportunity to develop in an organized, structured, competitive, and supervised environment. Teen hockey is for players who are 13 to 19 years old and want to learn and improve their hockey skills.

Professional Program

The purpose of senior hockey is essentially two-fold: Provide an opportunity for players in this age group to play organized hockey. Senior hockey is for players who are 18 years or older and play for fun or competition.
